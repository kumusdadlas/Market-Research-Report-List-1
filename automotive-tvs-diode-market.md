<p><ul><li>Uni-polar TVS</li><li>Bi-polar TVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive TVS (Transient Voltage Suppressor) diode market comprises two primary types: uni-polar and bi-polar TVS diodes. Uni-polar TVS diodes are designed to protect against positive voltage transients, making them ideal for applications where only one polarity is present. In contrast, bi-polar TVS diodes can handle both positive and negative voltage spikes, offering broader protection for circuits exposed to fluctuating voltages. Both types play crucial roles in safeguarding automotive electronics from voltage surges, ensuring reliability and functionality.</p></p>
