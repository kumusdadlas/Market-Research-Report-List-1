<p><p>The AMR current sensor market comprises two primary types: closed loop and open loop sensors. Closed loop AMR current sensors utilize feedback mechanisms to enhance accuracy and minimize errors, making them suitable for precise applications. In contrast, open loop AMR current sensors measure magnetic fields without feedback systems, providing simpler designs and cost-effectiveness, but with reduced accuracy. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, industrial automation, and renewable energy, catering to various current measurement needs.</p></p>
