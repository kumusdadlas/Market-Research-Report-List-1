<p><p>The automatic rotary ring stretch wrapper market encompasses two primary types: horizontal and vertical wrappers. Horizontal wrappers operate by moving the product along a horizontal plane, where the film is applied as the load rotates. This design is ideal for wider, heavier loads. In contrast, vertical wrappers feature a design that lifts products vertically as they are wrapped, making them suitable for taller, unstable items. Both types enhance efficiency in pallet wrapping, ensuring secure packaging and minimizing material usage.</p></p>
