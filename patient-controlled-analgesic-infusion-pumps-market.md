<p><ul><li>Syringe-Type</li><li>Cassette-Based Fluid Delivery Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Patient-controlled analgesic infusion pumps allow patients to manage their pain medication delivery. The market primarily includes two types: syringe-type and cassette-based fluid delivery systems. Syringe-type pumps utilize a standard syringe to deliver a precise dose of medication, enabling easy integration and monitoring. In contrast, cassette-based fluid delivery systems involve pre-filled, disposable cassettes that provide a controlled flow of medication, offering flexibility and often incorporating advanced features for dosage accuracy and safety. Both types enhance patient autonomy and comfort during treatment.</p></p>
