<p><p>The Damp Heat Disinfection Sterilizer market includes two primary types: Bottom Exhaust Sterilizers and Prevacuum Sterilizers. Bottom Exhaust Sterilizers operate by removing air from the sterilization chamber through a bottom exhaust mechanism, ensuring effective steam penetration and moisture distribution. In contrast, Prevacuum Sterilizers utilize a vacuum system to eliminate air before steam introduction, enhancing sterilization efficiency and allowing for quicker cycles. Both types are essential in healthcare and laboratory settings, ensuring the safe and effective sterilization of medical instruments.</p></p>
