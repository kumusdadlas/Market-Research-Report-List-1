<p><p>Telematics for on-highway vehicles encompasses three primary market types: embedded, integrated, and tethered systems. Embedded systems are built directly into the vehicle’s architecture, offering seamless communication and data management. Integrated systems combine telematics with existing vehicle components, enhancing functionality while maintaining some independence. Tethered systems rely on external devices, such as smartphones or tablets, to connect to the vehicle, facilitating data access and communication but requiring additional hardware. Each type serves distinct needs in vehicle connectivity and management.</p></p>
