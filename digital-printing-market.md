<p><strong>The Digital Printing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aqueous</li><li>Solvent</li><li>UV-curable</li><li>Latex</li><li>Dye Sublimation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital printing encompasses various market types, each with unique characteristics. Aqueous printing uses water-based inks, suitable for indoor applications with vibrant colors. Solvent printing employs organic solvents, ideal for outdoor signage due to its durability. UV-curable printing utilizes ultraviolet light to cure inks instantly, offering high-quality results on diverse materials. Latex printing, also water-based, is eco-friendly and versatile for both indoor and outdoor uses. Dye sublimation involves transferring dye onto materials, often used for textiles and promotional products due to its vivid, long-lasting prints.</p></p>
